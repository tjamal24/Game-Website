How to Play

Get as close to 21 as you can without going over, if you go over, you lose. Aces count as either 1, or 11, whatever's better for you. If your total is bigger than the dealers, you win. to draw another card, press the hit button. to stop drawing and see what the dealer gets, press stand. If the dealers totalis less than 16 the dealer will hit.

To start the game, make a bet.
